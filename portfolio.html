Minimalist drawing

Minimalist drawing

What is minimalist drawing ? Minimalist drawing is a method that allows a few simple pencil strokes to reveal a face, hands ... I've been practicing this kind of drawing for about 4 to 5 months. For a drawing I spend on average 4 to 5 hours, I am really demanding to myself and I want it to be perfect before doing the lines with the marker. You might think it's easy, but when you're not used to drawing, it becomes a complex task, especially when you have to draw with your hands what is really one of the most difficult parts of the human body to draw.

Roronoa Zoro

Roronoa Zoro

This is a drawing of Roronoa Zoro, a character of the famous manga One Piece.
Who is Roronoa Zoro ?
Roronoa Zoro the Pirates Hunter is a pirate, an ex-pirates hunter and one of the protagonists of One Piece. He was the first member to join the Straw Hat Crew, he is the first and main swordsman. His ambition is to become the best swordsman in the world, and he has shown an iron will to achieve it. He is a master swordsman who can use several styles of combat, whose particularity lies in the number of swords. His combat abilities are such that he sometimes gives the impression to people outside the crew that he is the real Captain.

Kakashi Hatake

Kakashi Hatake

This is a drawing of Kakshi Hatake, a character of the manga Naruto.

Who is Kakashi Hatake ?
Kakashi Hatake (はたけカカシ, Hatake Kakashi) is a jonin from the hidden village of Konoha. He is the leader of Team 7 composed of the three main characters of the manga: Naruto Uzumaki, Sakura Haruno and Sasuke Uchiwa. He is known worldwide in the ninja world for his use of the Sharingan (a pupil that normally only the Uchiwa family possesses and which allows to copy techniques and to throw powerful techniques of illusion) , under the nickname of "Ninja Copier".

Team fight Tactic is a strategy game where you have to create a team with characters from the league of legend.
